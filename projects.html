Hackathon Registration
Personal Project

This application was initally created for registration and check-in for Digital Women's annual Hackathon, Code Camp. The main goal of this application was to help make the check-in process easier. Previously, we used Major League Hacking's registration form, which had all registrants just in a excel sheet. We then had a hard time searching the excel when peole checked in and keeping count of the amount of people who had checked in and who hadn't. In addition to this issue, people were allowed to sign-up throughout the whole event. By just having it in an excel file, all the proper information we needed wasn't always filled out by those who were running the check-in table. By having an application for this instead it made getting the correct information when people signed up easier and also made keeping track of the amount of people who checked in.

Skills or knowledge gained: Typeform, Zapier, Google Sheets API, Heroku
